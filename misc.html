(About) "What is this?"

Are you sick of anime in general but still want something to read or watch? Well, you’re in luck because I also have non-anime recommendations for series! Shortened from the word miscellaneous, the “misc” page of this website is consisting of recommendations of shows or series that aren’t strictly anime, whether they be comics, cartoons or books.

Literally speaking, the term “anime” refers to any work of animation. Though in the lingo of American watchers, it specifically refers to any work of animation within Japan. Anime are usually, though not always, adaptations of japanese comics called “manga.”
